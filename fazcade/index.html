Fazcade

For an arcade, Fazcade is unusually big. It contains three floors connected with spiral staircases, with a bridge branching across the third floor, and numerous arcade machines. There are karaoke rooms located on the third floor along with the security office. Located on the first floor is a big DJ arena and booth, which is where DJ Music Man sleeps while he isn't manning it. There are numerous circular holes of any size along the walls and ceiling for the big DJ to crawl into to catch Gregory.
