Fazerblast

Fazer Blast is a large Laser Tag arena, consisting of a reception area, two or possibly many lobbies, and the arena itself.

It is where the Instructor Bot delivers a speech of the rules & regulations of Fazer Blast, then an armory where you equip yourself. You then go forward, with 3 doors. The one on the left takes you to the orange hallway, the center one is the exit from the Superstar Lounge where you receive your prize, & the right one is possibly for the other team.
