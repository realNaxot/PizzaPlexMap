Roxy Raceway

Roxy Raceway is a large indoor go-kart track and one of the many locations inside of Freddy Fazbear's Mega Pizzaplex. It appears in Five Nights at Freddy's: Security Breach, and is themed around Roxanne Wolf.

The hallway leading into the area is decorated with speakers, tires, trophies and motorcycles. The raceway itself is a wild-west themed go-kart track decorated with neon purple lights. The Glamrock Beauty Salon is also located here.
